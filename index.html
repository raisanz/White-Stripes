About Our Team

We are the White-Stripes Robotics team, our mission is to create a robot that will sort boxes at a warehouse using AI.

RoboFest Regional Competition

This season, we are competing in the regional RoboFest event in Lutz, FL. We are focused on building a robot that can sort packages in a warehouse setting. Our robot will acheive this goal by using AI for reasoning, Vex IQ for ease of building, and a bit of DIY to enable our robot to talk to AI.
